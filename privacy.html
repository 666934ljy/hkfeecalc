Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Information We Collect

HKFeeCalc is a static calculator tool. We do not collect, store, or process any personal data. All calculations are performed in your browser using client-side JavaScript. No trade data, input values, or personal identifiers are transmitted to any server.

2. Cookies & Local Storage

This site does not use cookies for tracking or personalization. The fee calculator stores your last-used inputs in your browser's localStorage so you do not lose your settings when you return. This data never leaves your device. Third-party services we may link to (broker affiliate links, analytics tools if added) may set their own cookies — please refer to their respective privacy policies.

3. Affiliate Tracking

Some links on this site to external brokers are affiliate links. When you click one and open an account, the broker may place a cookie to track the referral. We may earn a commission at no additional cost to you. We only link to brokers we have researched and believe offer competitive rates.

4. Analytics (Optional)

If we add analytics (e.g., Google Analytics, Plausible, or Cloudflare Web Analytics), this policy will be updated. You can opt out of analytics tracking via your browser settings or browser extensions.

5. No Server-Side Processing

All fee calculations run entirely in your browser using JavaScript. No trade data, input values, stock prices, or personal identifiers are ever sent to any server owned or operated by HKFeeCalc. The site is hosted as static files on GitHub Pages.

6. Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

Since we collect no personal data, there is no data to access, correct, delete, or port. If you have concerns about data practices, contact us using the information below.

7. Children’s Privacy (COPPA)

This site is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

8. Third-Party Links

This site links to external broker websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices, data collection, or content. Review each broker’s privacy policy before providing personal information.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated “Last updated” date.

10. Contact

For questions about this policy, contact us at: privacy@hkfeecalc.com
